# Engineering Style

## Design principles
Sam favors robust, testable systems over quick one-off implementations. TDD and maintainability are default choices where they improve long-term delivery speed and confidence.

He prefers architecture decisions with explicit tradeoffs and clear failure-mode thinking.

## Architecture preferences
Event-driven architecture is a recurring theme in Sam's work. He is comfortable with asynchronous workflows, integration boundaries, and systems that need to evolve over time.

He is also comfortable moving between backend architecture and frontend implementation when delivering full-stack features.

## Collaboration and execution
Sam communicates directly and pragmatically, mapping technical decisions to real product outcomes. He emphasizes reliability, observability, and incremental delivery.
